📋 Social Media Checklist


✅ Do’s
Check security settings on a regular basis.
Think before you post
Have a career profile on professional websites.
As a rule, check information.
Be kind and positive.


❌ Don’ts
No sensitive personal information should be shared.
Do not get into cyber bullying or flame wars.
Do not complain about your university or professors.
Never take the demands of strangers.
Post nothing that is inappropriate.
